Berry's Under Belly


SUMMARY

  • Role: VFX / UI Programmer
  • Status: Completed
  • Team members: 7
  • Duration: 2 Days
  • Jam Theme/Prerequisite: Cats/Only one mechanic
  • Engine: Unity

Description

You adopt a cat from the shelter, but something is odd... The foundation that you adopted Berry started sending you surveys, and every time you try to contact the admin, they only direct you to the survey...


Screenshots


My Contribution

Our game was relatively simple, so there was not as much to do. I chose to focus on the UI/design side of the game. We worked together on ironing out the design of the game.

  • Visual - Shaders(Outline, CRT), Post Processing
  • UI - Menu, Scene, Settings

Reflection

With a small scope and a brief timeframe, I decided to focus on UI and Visuals. I created a few simple shaders and played around with Post Processing. It was a good practice to learn/implement some simple shaders to enhance the game experience (CRT, Chromatic Aberration) We wanted the game to be a short simple retro thriller/horror game, and with the number of artists we had, I think we managed to produce a good feeling of it.

I did little programming since we had to split up the work between three programmers. But overall, we are happy with the results. We definitely had to cut a few features/visual implementations out of the game due to time constraint, but we did pretty well in the game voting.


Features that were implemented

Shaders
  • Inverse Hull Method, Chromatic Aberration.
