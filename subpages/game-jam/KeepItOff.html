Keep It Off



SUMMARY

  • Duration: 7 Days
  • Jam Theme: Light
  • Status: Completed
  • Role: Artist / VFX / Cinematic
  • Team members: 3

Description

You play as a kid going on a road trip with your dad. But something is amiss with the car...
Keep It Off is a 3D retro horror/thriller game with a simple mouse controls.

Contribution

Our team did not have any artist, so we had to wear multiple hats around the game. I chose to focus on the art/design side of the game.

  • Art - Car Model
  • Visual - Shaders(Outline, CRT, Glitch), Pixelated Render, Post Processing
  • Storyboard & Cinematic - Tutorial + Introduction

Reflection

We joined the game without any artist, and with the gameplay being relatively simple, we had to split up our contribution into different roles. I chose to focus on the art/design side of the game since I wanted to practice my skills.

I modeled the car and polished up the gameplay flow. I also wanted to try out more Shaders and created a few. I also explored creating a cinematic for the first time. It was relatively easy to pick up, and it did a lot in introducing the narrative and the gameplay to the player. We were close in winning but overall we are thrilled with the final product.
