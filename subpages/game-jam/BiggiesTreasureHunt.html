Biggie's Treasure Hunt


SUMMARY

  • Role: UI Programmer/Level Design
  • Status: Completed
  • Team members: 4
  • Duration: 3 Days
  • Jam Theme: Hyper-casual
  • Engine: Unity

Achievement


Description

Simple hyper-casual game made for RedGameJam2024. Drag and shoot biggie in KLIA to get AirAsia coins by bumping into various things!


Screenshots


My Contribution

I worked as the UI Programmer and the Level Designer.

  • Level Design - Tilemap
  • UI - Scene, Menu, Settings, Tutorial

Reflection

The gameplay mechanics were straightforward due to the nature of hyper-casual games, and the art design had to incorporate AirAsia's mascot into it. This was my first game jam in a while, and it was a good starting point. It plays like a pinball game but moving around the map to collect points. We previously drafted out the gameplay to have a few mini-games, but we decided to consolidate it to just one solid idea.

We were pleasantly surprised when we made the podium since there were many good games.
