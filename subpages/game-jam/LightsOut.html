Lights Out


<

SUMMARY

  • Duration: 3 Days
  • Jam Theme: Energy Efficiency
  • Status: Completed
  • Role: Programmer
  • Team members: 5

Description

A game about a parent trying to save electricity while their kid can't sleep...

Contribution

Our team was relatively inexperienced, so I was the main programmer. The teams were randomized for this jam.

  • Gameplay - Toggleable Lights. Interact System
  • AI - Patrol Behaviour to move between randomize light switches in the house.
  • UI: Scene, Menu, Settings, Tutorial
  • Tutorial - Introduction to teach the player what the goal of the game is.

Reflection

We had a pretty good idea that was unique but fell short in the voting to another team that had a similar idea. The main difference was the lack of experience in our team. But overall it was a good learning experience for everyone especially those who were joining a game jam for the first time.
