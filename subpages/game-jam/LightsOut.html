Lights Out


SUMMARY

  • Role: Programmer
  • Status: Completed
  • Team members: 5
  • Duration: 3 Days
  • Jam Theme: Energy Efficiency
  • Engine: Unity

Description

A game about a parent trying to save electricity while their kid can't sleep...


Contribution

Our team comprised students joining their first game jam, so I was the main programmer. The teams were randomized for this jam.

  • Gameplay - Toggleable Lights. Interact System
  • AI - Patrol Behavior to move between randomized light switches in the house.
  • UI: Scene, Menu, Settings, Tutorial
  • Tutorial - Introduction to teach the player what the goal of the game is.

Reflection

This was a pretty good experience since the teams were assigned randomly by the organizers. I had a few team members starting their game dev journey, and it was nice to give them a few ideas from art to programming to game design. I tried to give them a few tips and tricks they could implement during their projects, one of them being source control. We had a pretty good idea that was unique but fell short in the voting to another team that had a similar idea. Overall, I made a few connections and was happy with what we achieved.

Features that were implemented

Interact System
  • Abstract interface for interactable, and each interactable has its own concrete implementation.
  • Player raycast to check if within range of interactable.

AI
  • Simple State Machine with waypoints at each light, and executing The interact method.
