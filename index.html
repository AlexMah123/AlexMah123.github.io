More About Me!

Hello, I am Alex Mah, a programmer and graduate of the University of Wollongong, Malaysia. My journey into game development began after my foundation program when I decided to explore my interests. This leap of faith led me to game programming, combining my love for games as a kid with my passion for problem-solving.

Today I am mostly a generalist wanting to be more of a jack of all trades, but would like to transition mainly to be a game programmer. One specialization that I would like to explore more is Shaders. It has helped expand my view on how certain visual effects can be implemented to improve games.

Outside of work, I am a big competitive TCG player. I have played around seven titles and know the rules for two more, but I am currently actively playing Magic: The Gathering.
